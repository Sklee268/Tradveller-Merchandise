§ Our Story

Designed for movement.
Inspired by mindset.

The uniform of the new generation, born from the spirit of trade and travel.

At Tradveller, we believe life is a journey between two worlds — the one you build and the one you explore.

Born from the spirit of trade and travel, our brand represents those who pursue freedom with purpose — the thinkers, the doers, the creators who trade not just assets, but ideas, time, and energy to design a life without borders.

Our clothing is made for modern mobility: minimalist, functional, and timeless. Each piece blends performance and simplicity — adaptable enough for a long flight, a spontaneous trip, or a quiet morning of focus.

We value less, but better. Every material, every seam, every detail serves a reason — to keep you comfortable, confident, and ready to move.

Tradveller is not about luxury; it's about freedom — the freedom to work anywhere, live everywhere, and stay grounded while chasing the world ahead.

Frequently asked

The quiet questions, answered.

How do Tradveller sizes run?

True to size for a relaxed fit. The T-Shirt is intentionally oversized — drop-shoulder, longer hem. If you're between sizes and want a closer cut, size down. The Jacket runs true. Full size charts live on every product page.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, the Philippines, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Australia, the UK, the US and most of the EU. Rates calculated at checkout. Duties and import fees are included in the price for SG, AU and UK orders.

What's your return policy?

14 days from the day you receive your package. Unworn, tags on, original packaging. Email hello@tradveller.com with your order number and we'll send a return label. Final-sale items and personalised pieces can't be returned.

When do new drops happen?

We release a five-piece collection each quarter. Mailing list subscribers get early access 48 hours before public launch — that's the entire point of the list. Drops sell quickly; we don't restock.

Who makes your clothing?

Cut and sewn in Malaysia by a small workshop we've worked with since day one. Fabrics are sourced locally where possible — Penang cotton mills, Klang Valley dye houses. Smaller batches, longer lead times, but every piece is traceable.
